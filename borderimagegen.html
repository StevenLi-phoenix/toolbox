💡 Tips

  • Slice is measured from each edge. With percentages, 33% on every side cuts a 3×3 grid out of any source size.
  • Width unitless values multiply the element's border-width. 1 means use the border exactly; 2 doubles it.
  • Without fill, the center slice is discarded — the box's own background shows through.
  • round scales each tile to fit a whole number of repetitions; space distributes tiles with gaps. stretch is the default and often blurs.
  • The element still needs a border-style (anything other than none) and a non-zero border-width — border-image won't paint without them.
