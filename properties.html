Spec notes

  • Lines beginning with # or ! are comments (after optional leading whitespace).
  • Key and value are separated by the first unescaped =, :, or whitespace run.
  • A line ending in an odd number of \ is continued on the next line (leading whitespace stripped).
  • Escape sequences in values: \t, \n, \r, \f, \\, \=, \:, \uXXXX.
  • When writing, non-ASCII characters are escaped to \uXXXX if the ASCII option is enabled.
