About

Calendar units are not fixed: a "month" is anywhere between 28 and 31 days, and a "year" can be 365 or 366 days. The calendar breakdown walks the calendar one boundary at a time so the months/days come out right. The total in each unit figures use fixed averages: 1 year = 365.25 days, 1 month = 30.4375 days. All math runs locally — no server roundtrips, no time-zone tricks beyond your browser's local zone.
