About Pangrams

Pangrams are used to showcase typefaces, test keyboards, and practice handwriting because they exercise every letter. A perfect pangram uses each of the 26 letters exactly once — extremely rare in natural English. Shorter pangrams are harder to build; the shortest well-known ones clock in around 28–30 letters.
