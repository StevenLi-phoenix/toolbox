ULID Spec

Format: 01ARZ3NDEKTSV4RRFFQ69G5FAV — 26 characters in Crockford's Base32 alphabet (no I, L, O, U). First 10 chars encode a 48-bit Unix millisecond timestamp; last 16 chars encode 80 random bits. Max time: 10889 AD. Sortable lexicographically by creation time. Equivalent to a 128-bit integer, convertible to UUID.
