🔢 Number to Words

Spell out any number in English — supports integers, decimals, negatives, ordinals, currency, and check-writing style (“Two Hundred and 75/100 dollars”). Handles values up to one vigintillion.

Input

Commas, underscores, and spaces in the number are ignored. Use - for negatives and . as the decimal point.

Options

Output

Cardinal
Ordinal (1st, 2nd, 3rd…)
Currency
