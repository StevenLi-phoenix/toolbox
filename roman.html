Roman Numeral Converter

Convert between Arabic numbers and Roman numerals (1 to 3,999,999). Uses vinculum notation (overline = ×1000) for values above 3,999.

Reference

I = 1
V = 5
X = 10
L = 50
C = 100
D = 500
M = 1000
V̄ = 5000
X̄ = 10000
L̄ = 50000
C̄ = 100000
D̄ = 500000
M̄ = 1000000

Standard rules: I before V/X, X before L/C, C before D/M for subtraction. A character may repeat at most 3 times in a row (e.g. III=3, but IIII is invalid). The vinculum (overline) multiplies a numeral by 1000.
