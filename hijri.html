Hijri ⇄ Gregorian Converter

Convert between the Hijri (Islamic) and Gregorian calendars in either direction. Get the weekday in Arabic and English, day-of-year, day-of-week index, and a month-grid view showing Gregorian dates with Hijri overlays. Pure browser math — no server, no API. Algorithm

The tabular calendar uses an arithmetic 30-year cycle: years {2, 5, 7, 10, 13, 16, 18, 21, 24, 26, 29} are leap (kabīsah) with 355 days; the other 19 are common with 354 days. Within each year odd months are 30 days, even months are 29 days, except in leap years month 12 (Dhū al-Ḥijjah) gets a 30th day.

Conversion goes via the Julian Day Number: JDN(H) = floor((11y + 3) / 30) + 354y + 30m − floor((m − 1) / 2) − 385 + d + EPOCH where EPOCH = 1948440 (1 Muḥarram 1 AH = Friday 16 July 622 Julian = 19 July 622 Gregorian, JDN 1948440).

From JDN, recover the Hijri year via y = floor((30 · (JDN − EPOCH) + 10646) / 10631), then derive month and day from the day-of-year.
