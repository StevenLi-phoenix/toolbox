Why this exists

Conditional requests are easy to get half-right. This tool makes the validator visible, shows what clients would send back, and helps you reason about cache revalidation versus optimistic concurrency control.

Opinionated take: weak ETags are fine for cache validation, but do not lean on them for write safety. If you care about preventing lost updates, use a strong validator.
