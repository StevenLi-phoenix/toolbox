Formulas used

Ohm's law: V = I · R
Power: P = V · I = I²·R = V²/R
Derived: I = V/R, R = V/I, V = √(P·R), I = √(P/R)
Results are rounded to 6 significant figures. Values use SI units internally (V, A, Ω, W) but you can input with any prefix.
