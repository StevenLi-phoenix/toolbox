Format reference

Common Log Format (CLF): %h %l %u %t "%r" %>s %b
Combined: adds "%{Referer}i" "%{User-Agent}i"
Example: 127.0.0.1 - frank [10/Oct/2000:13:55:36 -0700] "GET /apache_pb.gif HTTP/1.0" 200 2326 "http://example.com/start.html" "Mozilla/4.08"
