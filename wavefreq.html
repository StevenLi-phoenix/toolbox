Formulas

Wave equation: λ · f = v (v = c ≈ 3×10⁸ m/s for EM in vacuum)
Period: T = 1 / f
Wavenumber: k = 2π / λ (rad / m)
Photon energy: E = h · f = h · c / λ,   h = 6.62607015×10⁻³⁴ J·s,   1 eV = 1.602176634×10⁻¹⁹ J
Half-wave dipole: L ≈ 0.95 · λ / 2 (velocity factor 0.95 for wire)
