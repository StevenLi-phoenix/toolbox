PR Feedback Prioritizer

Paste review comments, assign severity and effort, then get a ranked action plan. Built for the moment when a PR has 17 comments and only 3 matter today.

Dark UI Mobile responsive SEO-ready Single-file HTML

Quick formula

Priority score = impact × urgency ÷ effort. Higher score surfaces the comment first.

Impact and urgency are 1–5. Effort is 1–5, where 1 is quick and 5 is expensive.

Use it to decide what to fix now, what to batch, and what to park for later.
