Shoelace Formula

For a polygon with vertices (x₁,y₁), (x₂,y₂), …, (xₙ,yₙ):

A = ½ |Σᵢ (xᵢ·yᵢ₊₁ − xᵢ₊₁·yᵢ)|

The signed area is positive for counter-clockwise winding and negative for clockwise. The centroid is the area-weighted average of triangle fans from the origin.
