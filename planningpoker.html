Tips

  
    
    
  • Use the spread stat: anything wider than two deck steps means the team disagrees on scope — talk it out before locking.
    • 
    
  • The ? card is for “I genuinely don't know enough to size this”. The ☕ card is for “let's take a break”. ∞ means “too big — needs splitting”.
    • 
    
  • Take the median, not the mean — a single optimistic 13 shouldn't drag the team's 5 up to 7.
    • 
    
  • Everything is stored locally in your browser — closing the tab won't lose your session.
