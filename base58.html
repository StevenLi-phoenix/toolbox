About Base58

Base58 is a binary-to-text encoding used in Bitcoin, IPFS, and other protocols. It omits visually ambiguous characters (0/O, I/l) making addresses easier to read and transcribe. Base58Check adds a 4-byte double-SHA-256 checksum to detect typos.
