About Base45 (RFC 9285)

Base45 encodes every 2 bytes as 3 characters (and a trailing odd byte as 2 characters) from the alphabet 0-9 A-Z space $%*+-./:. These 45 symbols are exactly the characters QR code alphanumeric mode can represent at 5.5 bits each, so a Base45 payload fits ~30% more data per QR than Base64.

Reference: Encoding "AB" (0x41, 0x42) → "BB8". The string "ietf!" encodes to "QED8WEX0".
