How it works

The tool runs the Quine–McCluskey algorithm to generate all prime implicants, then uses Petrick's method to select a minimal cover of the required minterms. Don't-cares may be included in prime implicants but are never required to be covered. SOP (sum of products) uses 1-minterms; POS (product of sums) is derived from the complement via the same algorithm applied to 0-cells. Input variables are labelled A–E from most significant to least significant bit.
