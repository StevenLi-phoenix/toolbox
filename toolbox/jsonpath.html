📖 Common JSONPath Patterns
$
Root element
$.store.book[*]
All books
$..author
All authors (recursive)
$.store.*
All store members
$..price
All prices
$.store.book[0]
First book
$.store.book[-1]
Last book
$.store.book[0:2]
First two books
$.store.book[?(@.price<10)]
Books cheaper than 10
$.store.book[?(@.isbn)]
Books with ISBN
$..book.length
Number of books
$..*
All members (deep)
