Semver comparison rules: Major > Minor > Patch. Pre-release versions (1.0.0-alpha) have lower precedence than the release. Pre-release identifiers are compared left-to-right: numeric < numeric, string is lexicographic, numeric < string.
Bump rules: major resets minor & patch. minor resets patch. patch increments patch. pre-release appends or increments -rc.N.
