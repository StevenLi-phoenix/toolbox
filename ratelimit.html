💡 Quick Reference

  • Token Bucket: Best for allowing bursts while enforcing average rate. Used by AWS, Stripe.
  • Fixed Window: Simplest to implement. Risk of 2× burst at window boundaries.
  • Sliding Window Log: Most accurate. Tracks every request timestamp. Higher memory usage.
  • Standard headers: X-RateLimit-Limit, X-RateLimit-Remaining, X-RateLimit-Reset
  • IETF draft: RateLimit-Limit, RateLimit-Remaining, RateLimit-Reset
