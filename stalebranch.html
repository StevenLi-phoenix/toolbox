What the score means

guide

0–29: Fresh

Active work. Leave it alone unless it’s already merged.

30–59: Watch

Still relevant, but worth checking if it needs a rebase or merge.

60–79: Stale

Likely safe to archive or delete after a quick review.

80–100: Dusty

Probably a branch fossil. Prune it with confidence after verifying remote status.
