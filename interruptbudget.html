how it works
Interrupt budget
The planner converts your available hours, meeting load, and tolerance into a rough cap on how many context switches you can absorb before the day turns into mush.
Focus blocks
Higher tolerance means longer blocks; meeting-heavy days get shorter blocks and more buffer so you don’t lie to yourself.
Recovery time
Every interruption is taxed by your estimated switch cost, then cushioned with a recovery buffer so the plan is actually survivable.
