Constants & formulas

Speed of light: c = 299 792 458 m/s (exact). Planck: h = 6.626 070 15 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s (exact). Elementary charge: e = 1.602 176 634 × 10⁻¹⁹ C (exact). hc: 1.986 446 × 10⁻²⁵ J·m = 1239.841 984 eV·nm.

Relations: λ·ν = c  ·  E = h·ν = h·c/λ  ·  ν̃ = 1/λ  ·  E[eV] = 1239.84 / λ[nm]  ·  ν[THz] · λ[µm] = 299.792.

Boltzmann: kB = 1.380 649 × 10⁻²³ J/K. Wien constant: b = 2.897 771 955 × 10⁻³ m·K. Black-body peak wavelength: λpeak·T = b. Indices of refraction are 1 (vacuum); divide λ by n to get wavelength in a medium.
